What is Genshin Impact?

Genshin Impact is an open-world game and story-telling that was published in 2020 by company Mihoyo. The goal of this game is to explore the world, find treasure, fight with boss and meet with other adventurers. Many chacaters in this game possessed different kind of earth elements as a source of their pwoer. This game also focuses on leveling up characters.

There are 7 elements of the game which are Anemo represents wind, Geo represents earth, electro represents storm, dendro represents plant, hydro represents water, Pyro represents fire and Cryo represents ice. To get better characters in this game, you should get 5 star characters for their tremendous power. Ultimate goal for this game is to help the main character to find his lost twin.
