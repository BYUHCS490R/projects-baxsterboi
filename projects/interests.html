What is badminton?

Badminton is a sport that required you to hit a lightweight shuttlecock (or a birdie) with racquet over the net, aiming to the opponent's court to get a score point. This sport require you to have speed and fast reflexes.

Badminton rules

In this game, player should score the points by landing the shuttlecock in the opponent's court lane, with 21 points to win. During the serving, player should hit the shuttlecock underhand below the waist, no double hit and not touching the net. If the ball cross the line, it is consider out and the opponent score the points. After that, the player can move anywhere around the court.

Why I am interested in this sport?

I really like this sport because it required you to have a good strategy in order to defeat your opponent, which helps developing my intelligence. This sport also should be played in fast-paced and fast reflexes, which helps increase my speed. This sport is easy to learn even for beginner. I used to play this sport competitively when I was in high school.
