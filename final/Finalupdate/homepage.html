Introduction

Volleyball is a fast-paced sport and required player to have quick desicion making skills in order to win the game. This sport required a team to have six players. Each team are required to send the ball to the other court and hit the ground in order to score a point. This sport is really fun and simple to play.

In this era of globalization, volleyball has became widely popular in many countries such Japan, Poland, Phillipines and many more. Many people playing volleyball and some play competitively. Volleyball has been recognized as an olympic game in Tokyo in year 1964. Since then, many people around the world love to watch Volleyball in Olympic.

They are a many star volleyball players that are well-known today such as Robertlandy Simon(Cuba), Yuji Nishida(Japan), Zehra Gines(Turkey), Antoine Brizard(France) and many more. These people have became inspiration to other people who dreamed about becoming star in this sports.

Fun fact!

There is one Japanese Anime about Volleyball called Haikyuu, which inspired many youngters to play Volleyball.
